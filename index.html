About Us

Uyolo is a fun, interactive and secure online therapy platform providing a wide variety of tools; developed and geared towards everyone seeking affordable, accessible and convenient mental health services.

Our mission

Providing affordable, accessible and convenient mental health services to everyone seeking to garner habits that promotes positive affectivity and fosters healthy habits. Simply put, a life lived in virtue through self-reflection.
